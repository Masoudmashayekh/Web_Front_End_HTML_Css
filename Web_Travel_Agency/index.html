Iran is a land of ancient history, rich culture, and stunning landscapes—from the ruins of Persepolis to the vibrant bazaars of Isfahan and the poetic charm of Shiraz. It’s a country where tradition and hospitality meet timeless beauty.

Shiraz

Shiraz is the heart of Persian culture—home to poets Hafez and Saadi, the stunning Pink Mosque, and the ancient ruins of Persepolis. With its lush gardens, warm people, and rich cuisine, Shiraz is a city full of soul and history.

Shiraz

Shiraz è il cuore della cultura persiana: patria dei poeti Hafez e Saadi, della splendida Moschea Rosa e delle antiche rovine di Persepoli. Con i suoi giardini lussureggianti, la gente cordiale e la ricca cucina, Shiraz è una città piena di anima e storia.

Isfahan

Isfahan is famous for its breathtaking Islamic architecture, including the majestic Naqsh-e Jahan Square and the beautiful Sheikh Lotfollah Mosque. Known as “Half of the World,” it’s a city of stunning bridges, historic bazaars, and vibrant culture.

Isfahan

Isfahan è famosa per la sua straordinaria architettura islamica, tra cui la maestosa piazza Naqsh-e Jahan e la splendida moschea dello Sceicco Lotfollah. Conosciuta come "Metà del Mondo", è una città di ponti mozzafiato, bazar storici e una cultura vibrante.

Persepolis, also known as Takht-e Jamshid, was the ceremonial capital of the Achaemenid Empire and one of the most magnificent cities of the ancient world. Built over 2,500 years ago, its grand stone terraces, majestic columns, and detailed carvings reflect the power and artistry of ancient Persia. Today, it stands as a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of Iran’s rich historical legacy.

Tehran is Iran's capital and largest city, known for its culture, history, and modern landmarks like Milad Tower.

Chabahar

Chabahar is a port city in southeastern Iran on the Gulf of Oman. It’s strategically important for trade and offers access to the Indian Ocean.

Hamedan

Hamedan is one of Iran’s oldest cities, located in the west. It’s known for its historical sites and ancient heritage, including the tomb of Avicenna.

Mashhad

Mashhad is a major city in northeastern Iran, known for the Imam Reza shrine, making it a key religious and pilgrimage center.

Tabriz

Tabriz is a historic city in northwestern Iran, known for its rich culture, bazaars, and as a center of Azerbaijani heritage.

Bushehr

Bushehr is a port city in southern Iran on the Persian Gulf, known for its energy industry and warm coastal climate.
